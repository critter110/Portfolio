Solar Spectra Analysis

This experiment involved using a specrtometer to take several measurements of the light from the sun. From the thousands of data points (wavelength, intensity), Mathematica was used to fit a quadratic curve near the peak of the data. The maximum of this curve was used to estimate the wavelength of peak intensity and compared to known data. From this peak wavelength, the estimated temperature of the sun was calculated using the black-body approximation.
